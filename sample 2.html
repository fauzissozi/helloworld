TECHSERV COMPUTERS is one of the leading player in Information and Communications Technology. It origins date back to 2010 when two people came into partnership to start a stationery business in Kampala, Uganda. The business has grown through the visionary diversification in the IT sector for example stationery,e-services,seftware engineering,telecommunication,web designing and many others. We are proud to have attained local and regional recognition for provision of high quality equipments and services for example Desktops,Laptops,Printers,Scanners,Photocopiers,Networking devices, Electronics like TVs, Fridges etc, Services like Computer Repair and Maintenance, Computer Training, Software Installation, Software Development, Social sites Management, Web Designing and many more.
TECHSERV COMPUTERS ranks among the top tax payers in Uganda and employs a workforce of 200 employees, amidst ensuring the highest enviromental,health and safety practices are adhered to and maintained in all operational procedures. TECHSERV COMPUTERS is certified by ISO 9001:2005 (Reg:No. 0302010).
